Job Title: Legal Counsel

About Us:
We are a leading company in corporate law and legal advisory looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, striving to provide expert legal guidance and ensure compliance across all business operations.

The Role:
As a Legal Counsel, you will be responsible for drafting and negotiating legal contracts, providing legal advice on corporate matters, and managing intellectual property and regulatory compliance. You will work closely with Legal Department to mitigate legal risks and support strategic business initiatives. This role requires a strong commitment to legal integrity and strategic partnership.

Key Responsibilities:
- Draft, review, and negotiate a wide range of commercial contracts (e.g., vendor, client, partnership agreements).
- Provide sound legal advice to various internal departments on corporate, commercial, and regulatory matters.
- Manage intellectual property portfolio and ensure compliance with data privacy regulations (e.g., GDPR, CCPA).
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams (e.g., engineering, product, marketing).
- Ensure high quality, adherence to best practices, and timely delivery of projects/tasks.
- Continuously learn and stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and relevant regulations.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Law (Juris Doctor or equivalent) or equivalent practical experience.
- 5+ years of proven professional experience in Legal Counsel or a closely related role.
- Strong proficiency in Contract Law, Corporate Law, Intellectual Property Law.
- Demonstrated experience with Legal Research, Negotiation, Regulatory Compliance, Data Privacy (GDPR/CCPA).
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.
- Superior communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with State Bar Admission, Litigation Management, M&A Legal Support, SaaS/Tech Law experience.
- Relevant industry certifications (e.g., PMP, AWS, CFA, SHRM).
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, and evolving environment.
- A strong portfolio or demonstrable projects (where applicable).

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package (health, dental, vision).
- Generous paid time off and flexible work arrangements.
- Opportunities for continuous professional growth, training, and career development.
- A collaborative, inclusive, and supportive work environment.
- playing a critical role in safeguarding the company's legal standing and growth.
