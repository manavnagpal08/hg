Job Title: Sales Representative

About Us:
We are a leading company in B2B sales and customer acquisition looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, striving to drive revenue growth by delivering value to our clients.

The Role:
As a Sales Representative, you will be responsible for generating new leads and qualifying prospects, presenting compelling product solutions to potential clients, and negotiating and closing deals to achieve sales targets. You will work closely with sales team to exceed sales quotas and expand our customer base. This role requires a strong commitment to client focus and results-driven approach.

Key Responsibilities:
- Identify and qualify new sales opportunities through proactive prospecting, cold calling, and networking.
- Conduct engaging product demonstrations and presentations tailored to client needs and pain points.
- Manage the full sales cycle from initial contact to contract negotiation and closing deals.
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams (e.g., engineering, product, marketing).
- Ensure high quality, adherence to best practices, and timely delivery of projects/tasks.
- Continuously learn and stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and relevant regulations.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Business, Sales, Marketing, or a related field or equivalent practical experience.
- 2+ years of proven professional experience in Sales Representative or a closely related role.
- Strong proficiency in Sales Acumen, Lead Generation, CRM Software (e.g., Salesforce).
- Demonstrated experience with Negotiation, Presentation Skills, Client Relationship Management, Prospecting.
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.
- Superior communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Salesforce Sales Cloud, Salesloft, Outreach, Sales Forecasting, B2B SaaS sales experience.
- Relevant industry certifications (e.g., PMP, AWS, CFA, SHRM).
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, and evolving environment.
- A strong portfolio or demonstrable projects (where applicable).

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package (health, dental, vision).
- Generous paid time off and flexible work arrangements.
- Opportunities for continuous professional growth, training, and career development.
- A collaborative, inclusive, and supportive work environment.
- being a key driver of direct revenue and company expansion.
