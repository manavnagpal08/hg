Job Title: Data Scientist

About Us:
We are a leading company in data analytics and artificial intelligence looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, striving to extract actionable insights and build intelligent systems.

The Role:
As a Data Scientist, you will be responsible for building predictive models, analyzing large, complex datasets, and communicating data-driven insights to diverse stakeholders. You will work closely with data science and analytics team to drive data-driven decision-making and product innovation. This role requires a strong commitment to analytical rigor and business impact.

Key Responsibilities:
- Develop, validate, and deploy machine learning models for various business problems.
- Perform extensive exploratory data analysis, feature engineering, and data cleaning.
- Design and implement A/B tests and statistical experiments to measure impact.
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams (e.g., engineering, product, marketing).
- Ensure high quality, adherence to best practices, and timely delivery of projects/tasks.
- Continuously learn and stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and relevant regulations.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, or Applied Mathematics or equivalent practical experience.
- 3+ years of proven professional experience in Data Scientist or a closely related role.
- Strong proficiency in Python (Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn, TensorFlow/PyTorch), R, SQL.
- Demonstrated experience with Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Statistical Modeling, Data Visualization.
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.
- Superior communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Spark, Hadoop, AWS/GCP/Azure ML services, NLP, Computer Vision.
- Relevant industry certifications (e.g., PMP, AWS, CFA, SHRM).
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, and evolving environment.
- A strong portfolio or demonstrable projects (where applicable).

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package (health, dental, vision).
- Generous paid time off and flexible work arrangements.
- Opportunities for continuous professional growth, training, and career development.
- A collaborative, inclusive, and supportive work environment.
- working on challenging data problems with real-world impact.
