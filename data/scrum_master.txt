Job Title: Scrum Master

About Us:
We are a leading company in Agile software development looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our team. We value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, striving to facilitate effective Agile practices and empower self-organizing teams.

The Role:
As a Scrum Master, you will be responsible for coaching Agile teams on Scrum principles and practices, removing impediments to team progress, and facilitating Scrum ceremonies and fostering continuous improvement. You will work closely with Agile development teams to enhance team productivity and deliver high-quality software iteratively. This role requires a strong commitment to servant leadership and continuous improvement.

Key Responsibilities:
- Act as a servant leader to the Scrum team, coaching on self-organization and cross-functionality.
- Facilitate all Scrum ceremonies (Daily Scrum, Sprint Planning, Review, Retrospective) effectively.
- Identify and remove impediments to the team's progress, escalating when necessary.
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams (e.g., engineering, product, marketing).
- Ensure high quality, adherence to best practices, and timely delivery of projects/tasks.
- Continuously learn and stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and relevant regulations.

Required Skills and Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Project Management, Business, Computer Science, or a related field or equivalent practical experience.
- 3+ years of proven professional experience in Scrum Master or a closely related role.
- Strong proficiency in Scrum Framework, Agile Methodologies, Facilitation, Coaching.
- Demonstrated experience with Jira, Confluence, Conflict Resolution, Servant Leadership.
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.
- Superior communication (written and verbal) and interpersonal abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with CSM/PSM certification, SAFe framework, Kanban, Lean principles.
- Relevant industry certifications (e.g., PMP, AWS, CFA, SHRM).
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, and evolving environment.
- A strong portfolio or demonstrable projects (where applicable).

Benefits:
- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package (health, dental, vision).
- Generous paid time off and flexible work arrangements.
- Opportunities for continuous professional growth, training, and career development.
- A collaborative, inclusive, and supportive work environment.
- empowering teams to deliver exceptional software in an Agile environment.
